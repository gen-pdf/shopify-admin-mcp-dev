- `TOO_MANY_REQUESTS` — Too many requests.
- `CUSTOMER_DOES_NOT_EXIST` — Customer doesn't exist.
- `SAME_SHOP` — Target shop cannot be the same as the source.
- `INVALID_ORGANIZATION_SHOP` — Must be targeted to another shop in the same organization.
