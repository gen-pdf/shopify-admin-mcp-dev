# Enum: `OrderPaymentStatusResult`

The type of a payment status.

## Values

- `SUCCESS` — The payment succeeded.
- `AUTHORIZED` — The payment is authorized.
- `VOIDED` — The payment is voided.
- `REFUNDED` — The payment is refunded.
- `CAPTURED` — The payment is captured.
- `PURCHASED` — The payment is in purchased status.
- `ERROR` — There was an error initiating the payment.
- `PROCESSING` — The payment is still being processed.
- `REDIRECT_REQUIRED` — Redirect required.
- `RETRYABLE` — Payment can be retried.
- `UNKNOWN` — Status is unknown.
- `INITIATED` — The payment is awaiting processing.
- `PENDING` — The payment is pending with the provider, and may take a while.
