# Enum: `LengthUnit`

Units of measurement for length.

## Values

- `MILLIMETERS` — 1000 millimeters equals 1 meter.
- `CENTIMETERS` — 100 centimeters equals 1 meter.
- `METERS` — Metric system unit of length.
- `INCHES` — 12 inches equals 1 foot.
- `FEET` — Imperial system unit of length.
- `YARDS` — 1 yard equals 3 feet.
