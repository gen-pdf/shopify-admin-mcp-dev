# Enum: `ShopifyPaymentsBusinessType`

The business type of a Shopify Payments account.

## Values

- `CORPORATION` — The business type is a corporation.
- `GOVERNMENT` — The business type is a government.
- `INCORPORATED_PARTNERSHIP` — The business type is an incorporated partnership.
- `INDIVIDUAL` — The business is an individual.
- `LLC` — The business type is a Limited Liability Company.
- `NON_PROFIT` — The business type is a non profit.
- `NON_PROFIT_INCORPORATED` — The business type is a non profit (incorporated).
- `NON_PROFIT_UNINCORPORATED` — The business type is a non profit (unincorporated).
- `NON_PROFIT_UNINCORPORATED_ASSOCIATION` — The business type is a non profit (unincorporated_association).
- `NON_PROFIT_REGISTERED_CHARITY` — The business type is a non profit (registered charity).
- `PARTNERSHIP` — The business type is a partnership.
- `PRIVATE_CORPORATION` — The business type is a private corporation.
- `PUBLIC_COMPANY` — The business type is a public company.
- `PUBLIC_CORPORATION` — The business type is a public corporation.
- `SOLE_PROP` — The business type is a sole proprietorship.
- `UNINCORPORATED_PARTNERSHIP` — The business type is an unincorporated partnership.
- `PRIVATE_MULTI_MEMBER_LLC` — The business type is a private multi member LLC.
- `PRIVATE_SINGLE_MEMBER_LLC` — The business type is a private single member LLC.
- `PRIVATE_UNINCORPORATED_ASSOCIATION` — The business type is a private unincorporated association.
- `PRIVATE_PARTNERSHIP` — The business type is a private partnership.
- `PUBLIC_PARTNERSHIP` — The business type is a public partnership.
- `FREE_ZONE_ESTABLISHMENT` — The business type is a free zone establishment.
- `FREE_ZONE_LLC` — The business type is a free zone LLC.
- `SOLE_ESTABLISHMENT` — The business type is a sole establishment.
- `NOT_SET` — The business type is not set. This is usually because onboarding is incomplete.
