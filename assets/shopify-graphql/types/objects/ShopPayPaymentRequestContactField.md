# Object: `ShopPayPaymentRequestContactField`

Represents a contact field for a Shop Pay payment request.

## Fields

- `address1`: `String!` — The first address line of the contact field.
- `address2`: `String` — The second address line of the contact field.
- `city`: `String!` — The city of the contact field.
- `companyName`: `String` — The company name of the contact field.
- `countryCode`: `String!` — The country of the contact field.
- `email`: `String` — The email of the contact field.
- `firstName`: `String!` — The first name of the contact field.
- `lastName`: `String!` — The last name of the contact field.
- `phone`: `String` — The phone number of the contact field.
- `postalCode`: `String` — The postal code of the contact field.
- `provinceCode`: `String` — The province of the contact field.
