# Enum: `ShopifyPaymentsPayoutInterval`

The interval at which payouts are sent to the connected bank account.

## Values

- `DAILY` — Each business day.
- `WEEKLY` — Each week, on the day of week specified by weeklyAnchor.
- `MONTHLY` — Each month, on the day of month specified by monthlyAnchor.
- `MANUAL` — Payouts will not be automatically made.
