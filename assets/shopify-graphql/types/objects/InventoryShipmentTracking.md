# Object: `InventoryShipmentTracking`

Represents the tracking information for an inventory shipment.

## Fields

- `arrivesAt`: `DateTime` — The estimated date and time that the shipment will arrive.
- `company`: `String` — The name of the shipping carrier company.
- `trackingNumber`: `String` — The tracking number used by the carrier to identify the shipment.
- `trackingUrl`: `URL` — The URL to track the shipment.
