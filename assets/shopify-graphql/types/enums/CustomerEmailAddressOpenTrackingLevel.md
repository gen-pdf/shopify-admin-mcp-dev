# Enum: `CustomerEmailAddressOpenTrackingLevel`

The different levels related to whether a customer has opted in to having their opened emails tracked.

## Values

- `UNKNOWN` — The customer has not specified whether they want to opt in or out of having their open emails tracked.
- `OPTED_IN` — The customer has opted in to having their open emails tracked.
- `OPTED_OUT` — The customer has opted out of having their open emails tracked.
