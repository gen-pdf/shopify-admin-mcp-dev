# Object: `PriceRuleMoneyRange`

A money range within which the price rule is applicable.

## Fields

- `greaterThan`: `Money` — The lower bound of the money range.
- `greaterThanOrEqualTo`: `Money` — The lower bound or equal of the money range.
- `lessThan`: `Money` — The upper bound of the money range.
- `lessThanOrEqualTo`: `Money` — The upper bound or equal of the money range.
