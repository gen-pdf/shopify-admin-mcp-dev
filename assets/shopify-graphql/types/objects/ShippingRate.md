# Object: `ShippingRate`

A shipping option associated with order delivery that includes pricing and service information.

## Fields

- `handle`: `String!` — Human-readable unique identifier for this shipping rate.
- `price`: `MoneyV2!` — The cost associated with the shipping rate.
- `title`: `String!` — The name of the shipping rate.
