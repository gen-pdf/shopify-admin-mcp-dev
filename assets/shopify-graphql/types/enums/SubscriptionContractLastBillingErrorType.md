# Enum: `SubscriptionContractLastBillingErrorType`

The possible values of the last billing error on a subscription contract.

## Values

- `PAYMENT_ERROR` — Subscription billing attempt error due to payment error.
- `CUSTOMER_ERROR` — Subscription billing attempt error due to customer error.
- `INVENTORY_ERROR` — Subscription billing attempt error due to inventory error.
- `OTHER` — All other billing attempt errors.
