# Enum: `MarketingTactic`

The available types of tactics for a marketing activity.

## Values

- `ABANDONED_CART` — An abandoned cart recovery email.
- `AD` — An ad, such as a Facebook ad.
- `AFFILIATE` — An affiliate link.
- `LINK` — A link.
- `LOYALTY` — A loyalty program.
- `MESSAGE` — A messaging app, such as Facebook Messenger.
- `NEWSLETTER` — A newsletter.
- `NOTIFICATION` — A notification in the Shopify admin.
- `POST` — A blog post.
- `RETARGETING` — A retargeting ad.
- `TRANSACTIONAL` — A transactional email.
- `STOREFRONT_APP` — A popup on the online store.
- `SEO` — Search engine optimization.
