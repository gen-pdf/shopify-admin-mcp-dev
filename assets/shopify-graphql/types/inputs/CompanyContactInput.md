# Input Object: `CompanyContactInput`

The input fields for company contact attributes when creating or updating a company contact.

## Input Fields

- `firstName`: `String` — The company contact's first name.
- `lastName`: `String` — The company contact's last name.
- `email`: `String` — The unique email address of the company contact.
- `title`: `String` — The title of the company contact.
- `locale`: `String` — The contact's locale.
- `phone`: `String` — The phone number of the company contact.
