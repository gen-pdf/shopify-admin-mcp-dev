# Object: `UTMParameters`

Represents a set of UTM parameters.

## Fields

- `campaign`: `String` — The name of a marketing campaign.
- `content`: `String` — Identifies specific content in a marketing campaign. Used to differentiate between similar content or links in a marketing campaign to determine which is the most effective.
- `medium`: `String` — The medium of a marketing campaign, such as a banner or email newsletter.
- `source`: `String` — The source of traffic to the merchant's store, such as Google or an email newsletter.
- `term`: `String` — Paid search terms used by a marketing campaign.
