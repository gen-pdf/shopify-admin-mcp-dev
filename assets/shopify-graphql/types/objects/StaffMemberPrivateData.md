# Object: `StaffMemberPrivateData`

Represents the data used to customize the Shopify admin experience for a logged-in staff member.

## Fields

- `accountSettingsUrl`: `URL!` — The URL to the staff member's account settings page.
- `createdAt`: `DateTime!` — The date and time when the staff member was created.
- `permissions`: `[StaffMemberPermission!]!` *(deprecated)* — Access permissions for the staff member.
