# Enum: `MediaWarningCode`

Warning types for media.

## Values

- `MODEL_SMALL_PHYSICAL_SIZE` — 3D model physical size might be invalid. The dimensions of your model are very small. Consider reviewing your model to ensure they are correct.
- `MODEL_LARGE_PHYSICAL_SIZE` — 3D model physical size might be invalid. The dimensions of your model are very large. Consider reviewing your model to ensure they are correct.
- `MODEL_PREVIEW_IMAGE_FAIL` — The thumbnail failed to regenerate.Try applying the changes again to regenerate the thumbnail.
