# Object: `ShopifyPaymentsMerchantCategoryCode`

A MerchantCategoryCode (MCC) is a four-digit number listed in ISO 18245 for retail financial services and used to classify the business by the type of goods or services it provides.

## Fields

- `category`: `String!` — The category of the MCC.
- `categoryLabel`: `String!` — The category label of the MCC.
- `code`: `Int!` — A four-digit number listed in ISO 18245.
- `id`: `Int!` — The ID of the MCC.
- `subcategoryLabel`: `String!` — The subcategory label of the MCC.
