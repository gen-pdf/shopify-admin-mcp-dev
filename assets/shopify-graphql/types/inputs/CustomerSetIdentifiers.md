# Input Object: `CustomerSetIdentifiers`

The input fields required to identify a customer.

## Input Fields

- `id`: `ID` — ID of customer to update.
- `email`: `String` — Email address of the customer to upsert.
- `phone`: `String` — Phone number of the customer to upsert.
- `customId`: `UniqueMetafieldValueInput` — Custom ID of customer to upsert.
