# Object: `CurrencyFormats`

Currency formats configured for the merchant. These formats are available to use within Liquid.

## Fields

- `moneyFormat`: `FormattedString!` — Money without currency in HTML.
- `moneyInEmailsFormat`: `String!` — Money without currency in emails.
- `moneyWithCurrencyFormat`: `FormattedString!` — Money with currency in HTML.
- `moneyWithCurrencyInEmailsFormat`: `String!` — Money with currency in emails.
