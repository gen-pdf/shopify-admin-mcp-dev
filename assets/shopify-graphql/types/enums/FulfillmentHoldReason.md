# Enum: `FulfillmentHoldReason`

The reason for a fulfillment hold.

## Values

- `AWAITING_PAYMENT` — The fulfillment hold is applied because payment is pending.
- `HIGH_RISK_OF_FRAUD` — The fulfillment hold is applied because of a high risk of fraud.
- `INCORRECT_ADDRESS` — The fulfillment hold is applied because of an incorrect address.
- `INVENTORY_OUT_OF_STOCK` — The fulfillment hold is applied because inventory is out of stock.
- `UNKNOWN_DELIVERY_DATE` — The fulfillment hold is applied because of an unknown delivery date.
- `ONLINE_STORE_POST_PURCHASE_CROSS_SELL` — The fulfillment hold is applied because of a post purchase upsell offer.
- `AWAITING_RETURN_ITEMS` — The fulfillment hold is applied because of return items not yet received during an exchange.
- `OTHER` — The fulfillment hold is applied for another reason.
